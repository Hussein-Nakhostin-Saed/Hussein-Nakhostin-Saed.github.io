NSCaptcha

A .NET library for easy CAPTCHA integration

Supercharge Your Security in Just 5 Minutes!

Easy to Use

NSCaptcha is designed to be incredibly easy to install and use, allowing you to quickly integrate it into your projects. Just a few lines of code are all it takes to enhance your site's security.

Focus on Security, Not Complexity

NSCaptcha empowers you to protect your site against bots and spam without requiring specialized security knowledge. We've simplified the complexities of web security so you can focus on your site's content.

The Power of Customization

NSCaptcha is fully customizable. You can tailor the CAPTCHA's appearance to match your site's design, use your preferred fonts and colors, and even adjust its difficulty level.
